UBI Introduction


Usage-Based Insurance (UBI) is also known as pay as you drive (PAYD) and pay how you drive (PHYD) It's a policy that's priced based on everyone's "behavior."
Different drivers are given different premiums depending on their driving habits, making it fairer and more reasonable for safe drivers with low accident rates.
The cost depends on the type of vehicle used, depending on the time, distance, behavior and location.
